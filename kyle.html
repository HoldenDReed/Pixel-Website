Call of Duty: Warzone

Call of Duty: Warzone is a battle-royale type first person shooter. 150 players drop in and collect armor, weapons, and ammunition to compete to be the last person or team left standing. Built on the IW engine and released in 2019, it is currently one of the most popular video games today. Warzone is free to play! Click HERE to download it!

God of War

God of War follows Kratos and his son, Atreus, as they traverse the nine realms of Norse mythology on a quest to spread his deceased wife's ashes. Along the way, they come into conflict with Thor, Odin and other characters from the Norse mythos. The game received multiple awards upon release in 2018, and is considered by critics to be one of the greatest games of all time. God of War is available on PlayStation and PC, click HERE to buy it on Steam!

Red Dead Redemption 2

Set in 1899 during the decline of the Wild West, players step into the boots of Arthur Morgan as he runs from the law with his gang of outlaws. Forced to deal with the loss of the only lifestyle he has ever known and the collapse of the gang, Red Dead Redemption 2 is considered by many to be one of the best single player games ever made. It is available on PlayStation, Xbox, and PC. Click HERE to buy it on Amazon!
